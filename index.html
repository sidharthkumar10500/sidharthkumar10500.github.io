Sidharth Kumar

I am a Systems Engineer in the AI Solutions Group at Texas Instruments, where I develop graph compiler and custom node optimizations for efficient, low-latency neural network inference on in-house accelerator hardware.

I completed my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin, where I worked with Jonathan I. Tamir on generative machine learning and deep learning methods for medical imaging, particularly MRI, and associated inverse problems.

During my PhD, I also collaborated with Robert W. Heath Jr. on low-resolution DACs for wireless communication. Before UT Austin, I earned my B.Tech and M.S. from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, where I worked with Swades De on wireless energy transfer and UAV-based communication at the CNRG lab.
